Hopperoo is a new way of planning your next big adventure. By scouring the web for the cheapest and most time efficient route between your travel destinations, we take the headaches out of planning trips to multiple destinations. Based on your travel destinations and approximate durations of stay, Hopperoo looks at the different paths that can be taken to make sure that you hit all the sites you want, with money left over in your pocket for your Hostels happy hour. You no longer have to waste time manually entering and tracking different routes, let Hopperoo take the stress out of multi-point travel.

Initially Hopperoo will be limited to air travel and will have a hard limit on the number of destinations in a single trip. However, we hope to expand our offerings to allow greater flexibility by expanding to train and bus routes as well. We would also like to expand the number of destinations that can be part of a single trip. However, the algorithm will need to mature before that can be offered. The idea for Hopperoo came to us while planning our last big backpacking trip, so we are aware of the frustrations and stress of trying to make sure that you've planned the best trip possible. We also hope to ensure that our rates are the lowest possible, in order to do that we will be running an ad-supported site, rather than charging an extra booking fee, but don't worry, we won't sell your information to anyone.

What are you waiting for? Come travel with us.

FAQ

Why should I use Hopperoo?
We do the hard work of looking up different possible routes for your next adventure, big or small. Just tell us where you want to go, and for how long, and we'll find a route that's right for you.
What do you have planned for the future?
We are hoping to expand Hopperoo to include other modes of transportation, such as buses and trains. However, due to different systems in different countries, we might have to stick with flights for now. We would also like to expand the number of destinations possible for each trip.
